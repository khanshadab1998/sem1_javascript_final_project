About Us

Company Overview

Atithi Resort and Spa is an unique resort seated at the heart of the city of lakes, Pokhara. 'Atithi' is 'Guest' in English typically chosen to reflect the well nurtured Nepalese culture of revering 'atithi-guest' as cites the verse in Sanskrit ‘अतिथि देवो भवः (Guest is God).

Our Mission

Enveloped by the timeless embrace of Mount Fishtail and the serene Fewa Lake, our inspiration emanates from the captivating vista that surrounds us. We are dedicated to curating an unforgettable experience for our esteemed guests, combining the unspoiled allure of Pokhara with the legendary hospitality of Nepal. The majestic snow-capped peaks, the lush verdant hills, the invigorating mountain air, and the serene lake waters all converge at Atithi, offering a rejuvenating haven amidst the breathtaking Annapurna Himalayan Range. Atithi's architecture and facilities embody modern luxury. Our exquisite wild rosewood furnishings, meticulously designed swimming pool, invigorating spa, private Jacuzzi, and sauna rooms define a realm of opulence. We have meticulously carved a niche where sophistication meets nature, offering an oasis of relaxation and indulgence.

Our Values

  1. Cultural Reverence

    We hold dear the rich cultural heritage of Nepal and embrace the principle of 'अतिथि देवो भवः' (Guest is God). Our foundation is built upon the respect, warmth, and genuine care we extend to every guest, ensuring they feel like honored members of our extended family.

  2. Serene Harmony

    We believe in creating an environment where the natural beauty of Pokhara seamlessly harmonizes with modern luxury. Our commitment to preserving and enhancing this balance enables us to offer an oasis of tranquility and rejuvenation.

  3. Exceptional Service

    We are dedicated to exceeding expectations through impeccable service. Our team is empowered to anticipate needs, tailor experiences, and ensure every guest's comfort, leaving a lasting impression that resonates long after they depart.

Company History

Atithi Resort and Spa embarked on its journey in the heart of Pokhara, Nepal, a city known for its captivating lakes and awe-inspiring mountain vistas. Established 2010, our resort was founded with a vision to offer an unparalleled blend of traditional Nepalese hospitality and modern luxury. From its inception, Atithi was guided by the timeless principle of 'अतिथि देवो भवः' (Guest is God), a belief deeply ingrained in Nepalese culture. This ethos laid the foundation for our commitment to providing an exceptional guest experience that goes beyond the ordinary. Over the years, Atithi has evolved into a sanctuary where nature and opulence coexist in perfect harmony. Our resort has undergone transformations that have redefined luxury in Pokhara, with each enhancement inspired by the breathtaking beauty that surrounds us. The stunning views of Mount Fishtail and Fewa Lake have not only captured the hearts of our guests but have also been a wellspring of inspiration for our team. As we embraced modern architecture and amenities, we remained unwavering in our dedication to preserving the serenity of our natural surroundings. Our commitment to ethical stewardship and community integration has seen us engage in numerous initiatives aimed at supporting local artisans, promoting sustainable practices, and contributing to the well-being of the communities we serve.
